Interactive Article Graphic

\n

This project is a dynamic interactive article where background videos change based on the user's scroll position, with corresponding text overlays attached to each video segment. Inspired by the visual storytelling techniques in this New York Times article, I rebuilt the experience from scratch, focusing on smooth video transitions and engaging content delivery. In this project, I used Typescript, Javascript, HTML, and CSS. You can explore the live demo here: Nana's Graphic Xplore.
